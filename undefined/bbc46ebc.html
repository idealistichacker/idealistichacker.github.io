2025.12.24

How will you introduce yourself without any superficial markers? Please watch the video and make a speech about it.?

From my point of view today’s topic is a pathway leading to discover a real me without any easy-get labels such as hobby, personality, nation, religion, gender identity and so on. Then back to the key question—how I introduce myself to people without these superficial markers? My answer is that the paradox in your decisions、moods、and actions of your daily life is shortcut to exposure the real you. For instance, I am the one who feel restless after I show an indifferent attitude to the situation that my close friends are undergoing whether good or bad. But the next time I will repeatedly and unintentionally show the same indifference by my reply to them when they are confiding in me. Am I selfish and mean? If so, then why am I willing to listen and exchange thoughts with my friends constantly? The superficial labels here can’t work. They are inability to give a acceptable account to my conflict actions and moods. So, this is me— a perpetual paradox.

From my perspective, today’s topic serves as a pathway to discovering a genuine self, one not defined by ready-made labels such as hobbies, personality traits, nationality, religion, gender identity, and so forth. This leads to the pivotal question: how can I introduce myself to others without resorting to these superficial markers? My answer is that the contradictions embedded within your daily decisions, fluctuating moods, and recurring actions are the most direct revelation of who you truly are. For instance, I am someone who feels profoundly restless after displaying indifference toward the situations—whether good or bad—that my close friends are experiencing. Yet, the next time they confide in me, I unintentionally and repeatedly convey the same indifference through my responses.

Does this make me selfish and mean? If so, why am I consistently willing to listen and exchange thoughts with my friends? Superficial labels fail here; they are incapable of providing a coherent explanation for my conflicting actions and emotions. Thus, this is me—a perpetual paradox.

主要修改点解析：

  • 用词精准化：
    • “easy-get labels” → “ready-made labels” / “easy categories”。更地道。
    • “exposure the real you” → “are the most direct revelation of who you truly are” / “the shortest path to exposing your authentic being”。修正语法并强化表达。
    • “can’t work” / “inability to give” → “fail” / “are incapable of providing” / “are rendered useless” / “utterly inadequate”。使用更准确、有力的动词和形容词。
    • “constantly” → “consistently” / “steadfast”。对于“持续的意愿”，“consistently”比“constantly”（表频率高）更贴切。
  • 语法与结构优化：
    • 合并与简化句式：将“For instance, I am the one who feel… bad.”合并重组为更紧凑的句子，逻辑更清晰。
    • 修正标点与并列：将中文顿号“、”改为英文逗号“,”，并使用“—whether good or bad—”这样的插入结构，使句子层次更分明。
    • 强化逻辑连接：使用“This leads to…”, “Thus,”, “Therefore,”等词，使段落间和句子间的推进更顺滑。
  • 提升表达流畅度：
    • 将“the paradox in your decisions、moods、and actions of your daily life”优化为 “the contradictions embedded within your daily decisions, fluctuating moods, and recurring actions”。使用“embedded within”和更具体的形容词（fluctuating, recurring），使意象更生动。
    • 将“show an indifferent attitude”和“show the same indifference”分别润色为 “displaying indifference” 和 “convey the same indifference”，避免重复，用词更丰富。

What event or moment was the most unforgettable for you in 2025? And what is the thing you most want to do in 2026?

Happy new year! For me, the most unforgettable event was I gradually got adapted to live alone. In this year, the person that I loved deeply had gone far away. But there is always a piece of her inside me. Every decisive action and brave life gesture of her had a silent but profound impact on me. By learning how to say goodbye to her gave me a whole new courage to face difficulties that I had escaped in the past. In the 2026, I want to polish my professional ability to a high level until I beat down the sustaining emerged dilemma of living with this wisp of change that she left to me.

In 2025, the most unforgettable thing for me was that I gradually got adapted to living alone.This year I spent more time on myself. The person I loved deeply has gone far away , yet there is a piece of her in me always. Every decisive act and courageous gesture of hers left a quiet yet profound impact on me. Learning how to say goodbye to her gave me the courage to confront difficulties I had once escaped. In 2026, I aim to carry the change she brought into my life and keep sharpening my professional skills to overcome every employment challenge that comes my way.

  1. 时态与结构
    • 统一用一般过去/现在时；避免 “was I got adapted” 这类双谓语结构。
    • 把介词短语误作主语的 “By learning… gave me…” 改为动名词短语作主语 “Learning… gave me…”。
  2. 搭配与用词
    • “adapt to” 后接动名词 → “adapting to living alone”。
    • “a courage” 改为不可数 “the courage”。
    • “brave life gesture” → “courageous gesture”。
  3. 冠词与年份
    • 删除 “In the 2026” 的多余冠词 → “In 2026”。
  4. 逻辑与简洁
    • 将 “polish my professional ability to a high level” 简化为 “sharpen my professional skills”。
    • 结尾用 “carry the change she brought into my life” 准确呼应“她给我带来的变化”，并自然衔接“战胜就业挑战”。
  5. 文风
    • 保留原句诗意，同时确保英语母语者可读、自然。
